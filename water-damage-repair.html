Water Damage Repair

Suffering from water damage after leaks, floods, or burst pipes? Leigh Plasterers offers specialized water damage repair services to restore your ceilings and walls quickly and efficiently. From plaster water damage repair to full water damage home repair, our team in Leigh, Atherton, Bolton, Farnworth, Bury and beyond is ready to help.

Our Restoration Approach

We understand the urgency of repair water damage to prevent further structural harm or mold growth. Our step-by-step approach includes:

  • Assessment: Identifying the source of leaks or flood damage.
  • Drying & Dehumidifying: Eliminating moisture to stop mold or rot.
  • Plaster Removal & Replacement: Cutting out damaged sections and installing fresh plasterboard or applying new plaster.
  • Finishing & Painting: Achieving a flawless, painted surface.

Our methods align with best practices for water damage & restoration, ensuring a durable finish for your property.

Common Causes of Water Damage

Water damage can strike unexpectedly, often stemming from:

  • Roof Leaks: Damaged shingles or flashing lead to ceiling drips.
  • Plumbing Failures: Burst pipes, faulty connections, or corroded fixtures.
  • Flooding & Storms: Extreme weather causing interior water buildup.
  • Appliance Leaks: Washing machines, dishwashers, or water heaters gone awry.

Regardless of the source, we quickly address all water damage repairs to prevent mold and long-term structural issues.
