About Leigh Plasterers

Our Story

Leigh Plasterers was founded by a dedicated professional trained under WG Plastering—one of the most respected names in the region. With expertise in skimming and re-skimming, dry lining, rendering, and water damage repair, we serve homeowners and businesses throughout Leigh, Atherton, Bolton, and beyond.

Our mission is to deliver smooth, long-lasting finishes at fair prices. Whether it’s a small room or a large commercial project, we approach each job with care and precision—ensuring you get the best plastering results.
